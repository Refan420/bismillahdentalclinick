Our Dental Services

We offer a comprehensive range of dental treatments to meet the needs of your entire family.

Temporary Filling

A quick solution to protect your tooth until a permanent treatment can be performed.

Permanent Filling

Durable and long-lasting fillings to restore your tooth's shape and function.

Light-cured Filling

Advanced fillings that use a special light for a strong and aesthetic bond.

Scaling

Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar for healthier gums and teeth.

Tooth Extraction

Safe and gentle removal of a problematic tooth.

Deciduous Extraction

Extraction of a baby tooth to make way for a permanent tooth.

Root Canal

A treatment to save a badly infected or decayed tooth.

Tooth Capping

Protecting a damaged tooth with a durable crown or cap.

Teeth Bridge

A fixed dental restoration to replace one or more missing teeth.

Tooth Implantation

A surgical procedure to replace missing teeth with a permanent implant.
